About

Hey! My name's Maksym Dadykin. I'm 25 years old and I'm from Kharkiv, Ukraine. Before the war, I received a master's degree in law and worked as an investigator at the Antimonopoly Committee of Ukraine. But the war made its own adjustments and I decided to try myself in a completely different area. I've started learning Web-developing course on Udemy and I really liked this. Now I'm learning and practicing almost everyday. Then I saw SigmaSoftware on IT-Generation and I thought that it is a great opportunity to show my skills and knowledge. After the successful graduation I hope to become a part of SigmaSoftware's team. It's really important for me because I know Sigma is one of the biggest and strongest IT-companies in Ukraine where I can grow as a professional.
